! Sunset Hills by Glen

Instructions

  • Enter building heights as whole numbers, separated by commas.
  • Click the "Build them" button. The buildings will be built.
  • Click the "Who can see me" button to show which buildings can see the sunset.

After clicking the "Who can see me" button, buildings that can see the sunset will be a solid opaque color. Buildings that cannot see the sunset will be lighter and more transparent.
